About Us

Al Fadhli Leading General Contracting Company is a wholly owned and registered Saudi Arabian company, headquartered in Al Khobar, Eastern Province. We specialize in delivering integrated engineering and construction solutions across general contracting and civil works.

We are committed to maintaining high standards of quality, focusing on accuracy, professionalism, and timely project delivery at every stage. Our approach is built on careful planning, efficient execution, and attention to detail.

Our goal is to transform engineering concepts and plans into successful, real-world projects that meet client needs and expectations. With a flexible operational structure and a growing network of technical resources, Al Fadhli is well-positioned to support projects across the Kingdom.
